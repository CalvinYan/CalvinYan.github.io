Hi Mom

Hey, you found my easter egg! Either you're my mother, who is the only person I entrusted with this secret, or you are uncannily dedicated to sifting through the code of some rando's personal website. You're looking at Homemade Space Invaders, a final project I created for one of my classes in eighth grade. Flash game development was what brought me into the world of computer programming, and I regard this as the culmination of my skills throughout middle school. Being a twerp with no understanding of the importance of version control, I unfortunately lost the source code underneath the sofa cushions of time. But I hope you enjoy regardless.


Controls: Arrow keys to move. Press space to fire. P to pause.
