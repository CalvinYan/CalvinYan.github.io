Sitting less than a block away from UC Berkeley campus, Gypsy's Trattoria Italiana offers gourmet quality Italian cuisine for the cheapest prices around. Each plate can be customized by our customers since nothing is pre-made. Everything is made from scratch in the restaurant on a daily basis, including base sauces, stocks, and meatballs. Come in to experience the best Italian food Berkeley has to offer, and leave with a full wallet and an even fuller stomach!
